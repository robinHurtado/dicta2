Original Text:
There is a great variety of sports you can practice outdoors. Man uses the various natural resources for his recreation, not only on land but also on water and in the air. Different enviroments offer various possibilities for physical excersice. In countries like Switzerland, which has high mountains, hiking and mountain climbing are very popular, whereas sailing and diving are characteristic of islands and coastal countries. Sports are not a privilege of rich people. Some sports like golf, horseback, riding and hang-gliding demand money, time and special place, but others like cycling, soccer and jogging are common to everbody. Both old and young people enjoy sports. Football, Rugby and baseball require a lot of strength and good physical condition.
